# 📊 Nokhba Electronics — Financial & Sales Analysis

## 🎯 Executive Summary

A comprehensive financial and sales analysis project for Nokhba Electronics, 
a regional electronics distributor. This portfolio demonstrates proficiency in 
financial statement analysis, business intelligence, and executive reporting 
using industry-standard tools.

**Project Period:** FY2022 – FY2024  
**Data Points:** 120 transactions analyzed  
**Total Sales:** SAR 9.63 Million  
**Target:** Co-op Training Application at KPMG Saudi Arabia

---

## 📁 Project Contents

### **1. Excel Workbook: `Nokhba_Electronics_Financial_Analysis.xlsx`**

Comprehensive financial analysis with 6 interconnected worksheets:

#### **Sheet 1: Sales Data**
- **Records:** 120 invoices across 3 fiscal years
- **Data Fields:** Invoice ID, Date, Client Name, Region, Product Category, Quantity, Unit Price, Total Sale
- **Analysis Applied:** Data cleansing, standardization, duplicate removal
- **Key Finding:** Top clients are Al-Rajhi Banking and SABIC; Laptops are the highest-revenue product

#### **Sheet 2: Trial Balance**
- **Coverage:** FY2022, FY2023, FY2024
- **Accounts:** Organized by category (Assets, Liabilities, Equity, Revenue, Expenses)
- **Audit Procedure:** Debit/Credit equality verification
- **Key Finding:** Total assets grew 81% from SAR 2.1M to SAR 3.8M

#### **Sheet 3: Common-Size Analysis**
- **Method:** Vertical analysis (each line item as % of total sales)
- **Purpose:** Identify trends and margin stability
- **Finding:** Cost of Goods Sold stable at ~50%, indicating consistent operational efficiency

#### **Sheet 4: Fixed Assets Register**
- **Assets Tracked:** 8 fixed assets (equipment, vehicles, furniture)
- **Depreciation Method:** Straight-line
- **Columns:** Asset ID, Description, Acquisition Date, Cost, Accumulated Depreciation, NBV
- **Compliance:** Follows Saudi accounting standards

#### **Sheet 5: Payroll Register**
- **Employees:** 14 across 3 departments
- **Calculations:** Gross salary, GOSI deductions, net salary
- **Compliance Testing:** Verification of Saudi labor law requirements
- **Finding:** All payroll calculations compliant with regulations

#### **Sheet 6: Loan Scenarios**
- **Loan Amount:** SAR 1.5M for expansion financing
- **Scenarios:** Base Case, Optimistic, Pessimistic
- **Analysis:** Monthly payments, total interest, repayment terms
- **Tools Used:** Excel Scenario Manager, PMT function

## 📈 Business Insights & Recommendations

### **Sales Performance**
- **Strength:** Consistent 22.6% growth over 3 years indicates market expansion
- **Opportunity:** Gross margin remains stable at 50%, suggesting pricing power
- **Risk:** High concentration on 2 clients (Al-Rajhi, SABIC) — diversification recommended

### **Financial Health**
- **Positive:** Assets grew 81% (SAR 2.1M → SAR 3.8M), indicating strong reinvestment
- **Concern:** Monitor cash flow given rapid asset growth
- **Action:** Implement quarterly cash flow forecasting

### **Expansion Financing**
- **Recommendation:** Base Case scenario (4.5% interest, 5-year term) balances monthly cash flow and total interest cost
- **Alternative:** Pessimistic case extends term to 7 years, reducing monthly burden but increasing total interest by SAR 140K
- **Decision Factor:** Depends on projected expansion revenue and risk tolerance
